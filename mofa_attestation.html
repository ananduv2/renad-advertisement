MOFA Attestation

MOFA Dubai Attestation is a prominent legal process faced by both citizens and expats in the UAE. Top Attest is a trusted service provider well-versed in handling various MOFA Dubai attestation practices that differ according to the country. For countries lacking a UAE embassy, the documents need verification from the concerned country’s embassy in the UAE. Whereas, documents issued by the nations who don’t have their embassy in the UAE will require a notarization from the country’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Top Attest offers reliable MOFA attestation in Dubai services to authorize any legal documents credible to use across the UAE. With prolonged experience in the MOFA s attestation in Dubai, we provide our customers with timely completion and delivery of the attested legal document to their doorstep. Our team is adept and experienced in handling the documents with immense care and providing flawless assistance to track your file throughout the attestation process.

Certificates Required MOFA Attestation in Dubai

Requirements include the below mentioned certificates:
  • Educational Certificates

    Degree certificate, intermediate marksheet, matriculation certificate, private diploma, computer diploma, and technical diploma.

  • Commercial Documents

    Registration, Invoice, Partnership deed, and MOU.

  • Personal Documents

    Birth/Marriage certificate, Death certificate, Divorce certificate, Transfer certificate, PCC

Dubai MOFA Attestation – FAQs

  • In how many days will I get my educational degree attested from MOFA? ?
    MOFA attestation of education documents usually takes one to two days. But it is also completed in one day during an emergency.
  • Do I need UAE Embassy Attestation on my document to get it attested from MOFA? ?
    Yes, attestation from the UAE Embassy located in your home country is highly essential, after which the MOFA authenticates it.
  • Is it illegal to enter the UAE if I don't have attested documents ?
    You don’t need attestation to visit the UAE on a tourist visa. For getting a visa for a job, education, or establishing a business, you require attested documents.
  • My certificate doesn't carry attestation from my country; what shall I do?
    You can perform documents’ attestation through our trusted MOFA UAE attestation service. Get in touch with our expert and we’ll get you started with the process.
  • What are the steps to get medical documents attested in the UAE?
    Firstly, the certificate needs an attestation from your own country authorities, UAE Embassy there, and then proceed for MOFA attestation.
